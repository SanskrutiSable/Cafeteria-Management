Sneha Jadhav

I ordered from Burger Heaven through this app, and I was super impressed! The food arrived hot and fresh within 20 minutes, much faster than I expected. The burger was juicy, and the fries were crispy - just how I like them. The app is easy to navigate, and they have tons of options. Will definitely be using this for all my food orders from now on. Highly recommend!

Durgesh Patil

I tried Sushi World for the first time using this platform. The sushi was delicious, fresh, and packaged beautifully. The only downside was the delivery time. It took almost an hour to arrive when the estimate was 30 minutes. If you’re not in a rush, I’d still say go for it. Great food, but a bit slow on delivery.

Ketki Bhale
